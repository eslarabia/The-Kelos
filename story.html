story

Once upon a time in 1988, there was a young visionary who dared to dream big and create something truly special.

He gathered his passion, his skills, and his creativity, and set out to establish Kelos, a company that would redefine the art of glass blowing.

As he walked into the dusty workshop for the first time, his heart was racing with excitement and uncertainty. But he knew that he had a fire burning inside him, one that would drive him to greatness no matter what.

And so he began.With every day that passed, the team at Kelos honed their skills and pushed the limits of what was possible with glass. He experimented, he innovated, and he never stopped dreaming. And, before he knew it, he had become the market leader in Freehand glass making with the highest level of craftsmanship in Egypt.

As the years went on, Kelos captured the attention of the world with its stunning designs and impeccable quality. They were a force to be reckoned with in lighting fairs, home accessories, and markets everywhere.And yet after that, the team at Kelos never lost their passion or their drive to be the best. He and the team continued to explore new techniques and push themselves to the limit, always striving for perfection.

Today, Kelos remains a symbol of excellence in the world of glassmaking. Their stunning designs and innovative techniques continue to capture the imagination of people everywhere, inspiring them to dream big and pursue their passions.

And so, the story of Kelos continues, a testament to the power of passion and the beauty of creativity, reminding us all that anything is possible if we dare to dream.
