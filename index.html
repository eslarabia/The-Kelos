The Kelos Story

At Kelos, we take pride in our handmade glass pieces. We believe that the time and effort put into each creation is what truly makes them special. Our skilled team of artisans carefully craft each piece with their own unique touch, using minimal machinery in the process. This allows for a more personalized approach to each piece, giving them their own individual identities. When you own a Kelos creation, you can rest assured that you are getting a one-of-a-kind masterpiece that was made with love and precision.

Our Core Values

Tradition, Innovation, Sustainability

  • Handmade Crafted

    Each piece is carefully crafted by hand, with a deep respect for the time-honored techniques and traditions that have made Egyptian glasswork famous around the world.

  • Waste-Free

    we don't just care about crafting beautiful glass decorative lighting. We also care about our impact on the environment. That's why we're proud to say that our facility is completely zero-waste, with a commitment to using only sustainable materials and recycling every possible bit of waste.

  • Custom Designs

    At Kelos, we believe in the power of creativity and innovation. That's why we offer fully customized design services that allow you to create a one-of-a-kind glass decorative lighting piece that's tailored to your exact specifications. So dream big, and let us turn your vision into reality.
