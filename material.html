Material

We use Borosilicate glass and it is not your ordinary glass. It takes the power of boric acid to make something so innovative and unique.

The addition of boron creates a glass like no other, one that can withstand intense thermal shock without breaking a sweat. Its coefficient of thermal expansion is lower than any common silicate glass, making it the professional choice for those who demand the best.

Borosilicate glass is a cutting-edge material that combines science and practicality. From test tubes to cookware, this powerful glass is sure to impress anyone who values quality and innovation.

    Benefits of using Borosilicate Glass:

  • Temperature Range and Resistance
  • Visibility and Transparency
  • Easy to Clean
  • Superior and Durable
  • Environmentally-friendly
